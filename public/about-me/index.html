About Me

Mar 17th, 2012

I’m an Austin, TX based javascript developer at Bazaarvoice on the Customer Intelligence team.

I work primarily with:

  • Ember.js
  • D3.js / Protovis
  • Handlebars.js
  • requirejs
  • jQuery
  • Modernizr

Customer Intelligence is a large-scale HTML5 data visualization & data analysis application. We analyze and interpret product reviews and offer insights based on segment analysis, natural language processing and trend tracking.

Since releasing a v1, I’ve shifted gears to rapid prototyping future features and performing targetted user testing.

Before Bazaarvoice

Before joining Bazaarvoice I founded one startup, Press 9 For More Options, an A/B testing application & framework, and was the 5th hire at a now defunct Austin-based startup called eProoft.
